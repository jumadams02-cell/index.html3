About the Faculty

The Faculty of Science and Technology at Mutessa I Royal University is committed to excellence in teaching and research. We offer a wide range of programs designed to prepare students for careers in various scientific and technological fields.

Our faculty members are renowned experts in their respective fields, and our facilities include state-of-the-art laboratories and research centers.
